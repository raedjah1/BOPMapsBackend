BOPMaps

A Musical Geocaching App for Sharing and Discovering Music in the Real World

Swagger UI ReDoc Raw Schema

Key Features

  • Music Integration: Connect with Spotify and other music services
  • Geocaching: Drop and discover music pins on the map
  • Social Features: Connect with friends and share your music discoveries
  • Gamification: Collect rewards and compete on leaderboards

API Endpoints

  • /api/users/ - User management
  • /api/pins/ - Music pins
  • /api/music/ - Music service integration
  • /api/friends/ - Social connections
  • /api/gamification/ - Achievements and rewards
  • /api/geo/ - Geolocation services
